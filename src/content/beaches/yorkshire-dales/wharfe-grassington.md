description: An easy, pretty riverside walk from Grassington down to the Wharfe, with calmer pools by the weirs upstream that suit a paddling dog, opening onto a gentle meadow path toward Burnsall. One firm warning, though — do not swim at Linton Falls or under the footbridge, where there's a weir and a hydro plant and a young man has drowned. The Wharfe is deceptively cold with hidden drops; the golden rule here is never jump in after your dog — it swims better than you do.
