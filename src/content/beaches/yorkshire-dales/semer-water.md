name: Semer Water
region: yorkshire-dales
location: Raydale, near Bainbridge
description: The closest thing the Dales have to a beach — the largest of their two natural lakes (Malham Tarn is the other), a shallow glacial lake in quiet Raydale where a dog can run on the shore, paddle out a long way and swim in gentle water. No currents or waterfalls, which makes it the safest swim on this list for a nervous dog. It's a Yorkshire Wildlife Trust reserve with breeding and overwintering birds, so keep dogs on a short lead.
