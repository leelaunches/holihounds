name: Polzeath
region: cornwall
location: Near Wadebridge
description: A big, flat, gently shelving surf beach that's a family and dog favourite out of season — and off-limits to dogs in the daytime through the summer, as a Blue Flag beach with the longer ban. The gradient is forgiving for a paddling dog when they're allowed on, and the beachfront has everything. In summer it's an early-morning or evening beach; the rest of the year it's one of the north coast's best dog walks.
dog_access: seasonal
dog_restriction: Summer daytime ban (Blue Flag beach)
restriction_detail: Dogs banned 15 May–30 September, 10am–6pm under Cornwall Council's beach order. Allowed all other dates, and before 10am / after 6pm even in summer. Dates are reviewed yearly — check the beach signage.
safety_note: Surf beach with a tidal car park that floods — mind the tide both for swimming and parking. Lifeguard in season.
