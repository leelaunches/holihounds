# Verified Jun 2026. SEASONAL 15 May-30 Sep (Blue Flag band). Gwenver is the year-round alternative next door.
name: Sennen Cove
region: cornwall
location: Sennen, Penwith
description: The iconic one — a mile of white sand taking the full Atlantic at the far west of the country, with Land's End views and proper surf. It's an award beach, so the dog ban is the longer one, mid-May to the end of September, daytime only. Off-season it's superb for dogs; in summer, walk it before ten or after six, or just drop over the headland to Gwenver, which has no ban at all.
dog_access: seasonal
dog_restriction: Summer daytime ban (award beach)
restriction_detail: Dogs banned 15 May–30 September, 10am–6pm under Cornwall Council's beach order. Allowed all other dates, and before 10am / after 6pm even in summer. Neighbouring Gwenver has no ban. Dates are reviewed yearly — check the beach signage.
safety_note: Big exposed Atlantic surf — RNLI lifeguards in season; swim between the flags.
