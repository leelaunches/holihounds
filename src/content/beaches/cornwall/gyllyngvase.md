name: Gyllyngvase
region: cornwall
location: Falmouth
description: Falmouth's main town beach — sheltered, gently shelving, an easy walk from the centre, and dog-restricted across the long summer season as an award beach. Out of season it's a pleasant town-edge dog walk with a good cafe; it's more "dogs tolerated" than a wild off-lead beach. If you're staying near Falmouth in summer with a dog, Maenporth round the coast has the shorter ban and the better setup.
dog_access: seasonal
dog_restriction: Summer daytime ban (award beach)
restriction_detail: Dogs banned 15 May–30 September, 10am–6pm under Cornwall Council's beach order. Allowed all other dates, and before 10am / after 6pm even in summer. Dates are reviewed yearly — check the beach signage.
