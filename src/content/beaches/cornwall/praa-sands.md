name: Praa Sands
region: cornwall
location: Near Helston
description: A mile of gently shelving south-coast sand on Mount's Bay, warmer and more sheltered than the north-coast surf beaches and a favourite for a paddling dog when they're allowed on. It takes the shorter summer ban, so it's back to dog-friendly from September, and the dog-welcoming Sandbar sits right on the beach. Good for an easy off-season walk with soft sand underfoot.
dog_access: seasonal
dog_restriction: Summer daytime ban
restriction_detail: Dogs banned 1 July–31 August, 10am–6pm under Cornwall Council's beach order. Allowed all other dates, and before 10am / after 6pm even in summer. Dates are reviewed yearly — check the beach signage.
safety_note: Rip currents on the surf and rocky reefs at each end — swim between the flags. The tide covers most of the sand, so keep an eye on it. Lifeguarded in season.
