description: A sandy, rockpool-strewn pair of beaches on the Lizard's east flank, split by the Caerverracks rocks — and the split matters for dogs. The east beach is dog-friendly all year; the west (main) beach carries the standard summer ban. Walk the short coast path to the east side and you've year-round sand with rocks to nose around at low tide. Sheltered enough for families and a decent swimmer.
