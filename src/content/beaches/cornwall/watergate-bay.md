description: Two miles of Atlantic surf just north of Newquay, and — because it's privately owned rather than under the council's seasonal order — a beach that welcomes dogs all year, daytimes included. That's rare on this stretch of coast, and it makes Watergate the go-to when the town beaches close to dogs in summer. Wide open at low tide, a proper gallop for a fit dog; on a lead through the hotel's own frontage.
