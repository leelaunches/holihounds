description: The most set-up dog-swimming spot on this list — a National Trust Victorian park at the southern tip of Windermere, with a grass-and-shingle shore, a dog-friendly cafe and boat hire. It carries the Trust's top dog rating, with dogs on lead through the parkland and off-lead in the water under close control. The one real caveat is the lake itself, which is the most algae-prone in the district.
