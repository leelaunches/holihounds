name: Glencoyne Bay

location: Ullswater, near Glenridding
description: A shingle-and-rock bay on the west shore of Ullswater with superb views down the lake — but read the access line carefully, because this is the one spot here with a real lead rule. The National Trust requires dogs on a lead at all times at Glencoyne, for the wildlife and surrounding livestock, so it's a paddle-and-walk beach rather than an off-lead romp. Ullswater's Steamers take dogs for about a pound if you fancy the boat.
