description: A long golden beach below a pretty, pastel-fronted village at the mouth of the River Aln — dog-friendly all year, and handy for Alnwick, the castle and gardens. The sand runs for miles south towards Warkworth, the estuary gives a gentler paddle than the open surf, and the village is walkable for a coffee with the dog afterwards. An easy, characterful base on the mid-Northumberland coast.

restriction_detail: No summer dog ban — dogs are welcome all year. Take care around the Aln estuary channel, and keep dogs under control near the dunes and the golf links behind the beach. Check the signage on arrival.
safety_note: The Aln estuary has fast tidal currents and a channel that fills quickly — mind dogs near the river mouth. Open-coast rips and cold water apply on the main beach; no year-round lifeguard.
